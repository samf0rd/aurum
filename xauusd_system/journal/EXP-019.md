# EXP-019 — Session Filter (London + NY only)

**Date:** 2026-06-07  
**Status:** COMPLETE — NEGATIVE  
**Config:** EXP-010 + `session_filter=true`, research_mode=True  
**Data:** 2020-01-01 → 2025-12-31, 207,936 bars  

---

## Hypothesis

M15 gold has materially different microstructure across trading sessions. London (08:00-12:00 UTC) and NY (13:00-17:00 UTC) opens produce higher-quality breakouts than the Asian session (00:00-07:59 UTC) or post-NY afternoon (17:00-23:59 UTC), where thin liquidity generates noise-driven range breaks.

Sessions allowed:
- **London:** bars[-1].timestamp.hour ∈ {8, 9, 10, 11}
- **NY:** bars[-1].timestamp.hour ∈ {13, 14, 15, 16}
- **Blocked:** Asian session, London/NY midday gap (hour 12), and post-NY afternoon

## Analysis

### 1. False-breakout rate improved meaningfully

74.3% vs 79.0% — best false-breakout improvement of the three experiments (+4.7pp). The session filter genuinely does improve entry quality at the signal level: fewer Asian-session range-break reversals.

### 2. 2021, 2022, 2023 improved

| Year | EXP-010 | EXP-019 | Delta |
|------|---------|---------|-------|
| 2021 | -$9,017 | +$1,105 | **+$10,122** |
| 2022 | -$12,179 | +$7,263 | **+$19,442** |
| 2023 | +$13,822 | +$14,434 | +$612 |

The 2021-2022 improvement is striking. These years were gold consolidation/correction phases where the baseline system was bleeding from continuous failed breakouts. The session filter blocked many of the Asian-session noise breakouts that drove 2021-2022 losses.

### 3. 2020 and 2025 significantly degraded

| Year | EXP-010 | EXP-019 | Delta |
|------|---------|---------|-------|
| 2020 | +$14,434 | -$6,095 | **-$20,529** |
| 2025 | +$43,915 | ~+$14,000* | **~-$30,000** |

2020 March gold surge occurred in pre-open and Asian session hours. The session filter blocked the early breakout entries on the initial COVID flight-to-safety move. 2025's gold ATH run in April 2025 also started outside London/NY windows.

These two years represent the majority of the baseline's profit. Filtering them out causes the net to collapse.

### 4. Strip-top-3 PF = 0.91 despite better false-breakout rate

This seeming contradiction is explained by trade size distribution. The session filter blocks many small-gain trades (Asian session has smaller ATR → smaller winners), which disproportionately affects the right tail. The base distribution gets narrower, but the top-3 outliers are in precisely the sessions that were blocked.

---

## Verdict: DO NOT USE AS STANDALONE

The session filter has a genuine positive effect on entry quality for trending years (2022-2023) but catastrophically blocks the system's highest-earning periods (March 2020 COVID, April 2025 ATH). Net P&L is +$9,602 vs baseline +$34,178 — a 72% reduction in dollar terms.

Potential value as a COMBINATION filter with EXP-020:
- EXP-020 (H1 gate) already improves 2022-2023 and 2025 while leaving 2021 worse
- EXP-019 improves 2021-2022 but destroys 2020 and 2025
- The combination might improve 2022 (both filters would agree) while EXP-020's positive 2025 effect partially counteracts EXP-019's negative 2025 effect
- Risk: combined trade reduction could be severe (< 400 trades over 6 years)
