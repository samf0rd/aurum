**Date:** 2026-06-07

## Setup

EXP-010 (long_only=True, conf=3, d=20, ADX≥25) produced PF 1.08 over the full
2020-2025 window. The 6-year result contains two large outliers in the 2023-2025
window: Oct 2023 (R=+24.14, $20,854) and Apr 2025 (R=+15.16, $15,219). These two
trades account for $36,073 — more than the full window's +$33,658 profit.

Walk-forward split:
- **EXP-012a**: 2020-01-01 → 2022-12-31 (in-sample training window)
- **EXP-012b**: 2023-01-01 → 2025-12-31 (out-of-sample test window)

**Note:** A data_loader bug caused early runs to fetch only 5000 bars from the Twelve
Data API. Fixed by adding `_find_covering_cache()` to load+filter the broader dukascopy
cache. The fix is in `src/backtest/data_loader.py`.

## Interpretation

### What this confirms
1. **The strategy does not overfit to the in-sample period.** OOS performance is
   better on every metric. A purely IS-fit strategy would degrade OOS.

2. **The regime filter works correctly.** In 2021-2022 (gold declining/ranging),
   the system mostly sat out, limiting losses. In 2023-2025 (gold bull), it traded
   actively with high win rates.

3. **The false-breakout rate adapts to market structure.** In uptrends, long
   Donchian breakouts hold more often (72.3% vs 79% in flat/mixed conditions).

### What this raises as concerns

1. **The IS period is not self-sustaining.** Strip-top-3 IS PF = 0.85 (-$33,377).
   The 2021-2022 sub-period is meaningfully negative. The full IS result (PF 0.97)
   is rescued by three 2020 COVID gold moves. Without those events, 2021-2022 alone
   would show negative PF.

2. **The OOS base is barely breakeven.** Strip-top-3 OOS PF = 0.99 (-$1,442).
   Even in the strongest gold bull market in decades, 485 "normal" trades are flat.
   All the profit comes from 3 macro-event breakouts.

3. **The strategy is a gold-bull-market strategy.** Both IS and OOS periods require
   large structural gold moves (COVID recovery, Fed pivot, war risk, ATH breaks) to
   generate profits. In prolonged bear or ranging markets, the strategy will be
   approximately flat at best, meaningfully negative at worst.

### The honest summary

The EXP-010 configuration is a **gold macro-event capture system** disguised as a
trend-following strategy. The "edge" comes from:
- Correctly positioning long BEFORE large gold structural moves
- Using Donchian-20 breakouts as the trigger for these events
- The 25+ bar duration trades (where the strategy earns all its money) are the
  same trades that ride gold's big structural moves

The system does NOT have consistent daily/weekly edge from "trend following." It has
edge from being positioned correctly when major gold moves occur, with the Donchian
breakout filter as the event detector.

**Is this valuable?** Yes — capturing gold macro events systematically with
controlled risk (stops at Donchian-20 level) is a legitimate strategy. But it should
be understood as an event-capture system, not a momentum system.

**Risk for live deployment:**
- Long periods of flat-to-negative P&L (2021-2022 was approximately this)
- Drawdown of -35% IS period is from these non-event windows
- Calmar ratio (~0.14 full window) is low; needs M15-calibrated risk parameters
