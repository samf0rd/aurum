# EXP-001: Donchian-20 M15 long-only baseline

**Date:** 2025-11-01  
**Status:** REJECTED  
**Results file:** results/exp-001.json

## Hypothesis
Donchian-20 channel breakout on M15 gold, long-only, with ADX + volatility + SMA filters,
has a positive structural edge.

## Parameters
- Symbol: XAUUSD
- Timeframe: M15
- Profile: intraday
- Date range: 2025-01-01 → 2025-12-31
- Entry: close above Donchian-20 upper channel
- Filters: ADX ≥ 20, vol ratio gate ≤ 2×, SMA-200 trend filter
- Stop: ATR-based trailing (2× ATR stop, Donchian-10 ratchet)
- Starting equity: $100,000

## Results summary
- Trades: 85
- Win rate: 29.4%
- Profit factor: 0.47
- Net P&L: -$8,200
- Max drawdown: 14.2%

## Verdict
REJECTED. No structural edge. Key failure modes:
1. 48% of entries immediately retrace (false breakout machine)
2. Long side PF 0.31 — entire strategy is underwater on the dominant trade direction
3. Strip 3 outlier trades → PF collapses to 0.27
4. Only trades surviving 25+ bars are profitable — entry timing is wrong
