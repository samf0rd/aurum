# EXP-002: Entry confirmation delay — bar+1 must hold above Donchian-20

**Date:** 2026-06-06
**Status:** SUPPORTED (with caveats — see below)
**Results file:** results/exp-002.json
**Experiment config:** experiments/exp-002.yaml

## Hypothesis
Requiring the bar after a Donchian-20 breakout to also close above the band
will filter false breakouts, reduce immediate retracement rate, and improve PF.

## Parameters
- Symbol: XAUUSD
- Timeframe: H1 (Swing profile) — *intended M15 Intraday; no M15 cache available*
- Date range: 2024-06-06 → 2025-12-31 (same H1 cache as baseline re-run)
- `entry_confirmation_bars: 1`
- `donchian_entry: 20`, `adx_threshold: 25`
- Starting equity: $100,000
- Baseline: exp-001-rerun.json (same H1 window, `entry_confirmation_bars=0`)

## Key diagnostic findings

1. **Trade frequency fell as expected** (11.4 → 7.6 /month). Confirmation IS filtering —
   just not the specific false-retracer cohort originally targeted.

2. **Baseline was stopped by risk engine in Jan 2025** (−14.8% drawdown hit ~15% limit).
   EXP-002 survived the full 19-month period, ending at +$7,611. The "trade count doubled"
   observation was a sample-length artefact: 8 months of baseline vs 19 months of EXP-002.

3. **PF improved materially** (0.48 → 1.17). Strip-top-3 PF went from 0.28 → 0.88,
   above the 0.7 threshold for warranting further experimentation.

4. **Engine bug found and fixed** during this experiment. The during-bar stop check was
   running against the signal bar's OHLC after the position had already filled at the next
   bar's open, producing `bars_held = -1` on ~4% of trades. Fix: added `just_entered`
   flag in `engine.py` to skip the invalid stop check. Pre-fix PF was 1.05; post-fix 1.17.

5. **Short side remains the weak leg**: TRENDING_BEAR PF 0.61 vs TRENDING_BULL 1.49.
   Structural asymmetry inherited from EXP-001 — not yet addressed.

## Caveats

- **Timeframe mismatch**: experiment ran on H1 (Swing profile) due to missing M15 cache.
  The original hypothesis targeted M15 Intraday. M15 comparison still pending.
- **Long-only edge**: TRENDING_BULL PF 1.49 carries the result. Bear-side remains
  underwater (PF 0.61).
- **Three outlier trades** (R > 3.0) account for a significant share of gross profit.
  Strip-top-3 PF of 0.88 is better than baseline (0.28) but not yet > 1.0.

## Verdict
SUPPORTED. PF crossed 1.0 and the account survived the full data period where the
baseline failed. Mechanism confirmed: confirmation=1 filters weaker breakout events
(lower trade frequency), not specifically retracers at entry. Proceed to EXP-003.
