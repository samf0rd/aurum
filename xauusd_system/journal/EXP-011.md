**Date:** 2026-06-07

**Results file:** results/exp-011.json

## Result

Identical to EXP-010 in every metric: 936 trades, PF 1.08, +$33,658, Max DD -35.5%,
Sharpe +0.28.

## Finding

Raising ADX threshold from 25 to 30 filters zero trades. All 936 EXP-010 entries
already had ADX > 30 at the moment of entry. The TRENDING_BULL regime filter is the
binding constraint — it requires strong trend conditions that effectively enforce
ADX > 30 already.

**Implication:** The ADX threshold parameter (25–30 range) is redundant in the
current configuration. The regime classifier is the active gating mechanism.
Further ADX increases (35, 40) would be a different experiment but are unlikely to
add value since the TRENDING_BULL classifier already does similar work.

The EXP-010 configuration (`long_only=True, conf=3, d=20, ADX≥25`) is confirmed as
the canonical M15 baseline. ADX is not a meaningful tuning lever at the margin.
